Umakhi wesuku lwenyanga
GNOME unomganga wokuthuthukisa, wabahleli bezinhlelo zokusentshenziswa ezivumela ukwakhiwa kwezinhlelo ezilukhuni.
GNOME uphethe kwisiga-nyezi, njengomphathi hele, okulayaluza kwelwembu, uhlu, nezisentshenziso eziningi.
GNOME imahala, iyasebenziseka, iyangenela endwaweni yokusentshenzelwa ebusweni besiga-nyezi njengomndeni we UNIX.
ukusebenziseka nokungeniseka nokuyingiliza, nokusekelwa kwe GNOME kuyenze yaba ekhethekile kosoftware abakhululekiyo bobuso beziga-nyezi.
Amandla e GNOME asemandleni omphakathi wethu. Empeleni wonke umuntu, onokuhlakanipha kokubhala izinhelo nongenakho, angabanomthelele ukwenza ngcono GNOME.
Amakhulukhlu abantu anikele ukubhalwa kwezinhlelo ku GNOME kususela iqanjwa ngo-1997; abanye abaningi banikele ngezinye izindlela ezibalulekile, kufakwa nokuhunyushwa, ukubhalwa, nokugcizelela okuhle.
