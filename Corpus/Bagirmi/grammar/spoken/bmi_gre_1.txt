# special_characters:	The transcription is fully explained in Keegan & Djibrine (2016), pp. iii. Special characters include: <ǝ>: unrounded central mid vowel; <ɨ>: central high vowel; <ɓ>: implosive bilabial; <ɲ>: palatal nasal; <ŋ>: velar nasal; <ɗ> implosive alveolar; <š> palatal fricative; <ƴ> implosive palatal glide. The tone diacritics are <á>: high tone; <à> low tone; <ā> mid tone; <â> high low tone (contour). Some of the special characters for tone marked vowels are produced in unicode by using the "combining tone marks", these include: <ǝ́>, <ɨ́>, <ɨ̀>, <ǝ̀>.
