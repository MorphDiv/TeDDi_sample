Addendum
At its 84th plenary meeting, on 29 March 2005, the General Assembly, on the proposal of the Secretary-General (see A/59/239), decided to add the following item to the list of items allocated to the Fifth Committee under heading I (Organizational, administrative and other matters):
Financing of the United Nations Mission in the Sudan (item 164).
