Agenda of the sixtieth session of the General Assembly
Addendum
At its 40th plenary meeting, on 31 October 2005, the General Assembly, on the recommendation of the General Committee (A/60/250/Add.2), decided to include in the agenda of its sixtieth session under heading I (Organizational, administrative and other matters) the following item:
Observer status for the Ibero-American Conference in the General Assembly.
