Fifty-ninth session
Agenda item 107
Review of the efficiency of the administrative and financial functioning of the United Nations
Report of the Fifth Committee
Rapporteur: Mrs. Denisa Hutanova (Slovakia)
Introduction
The previous recommendations made by the Fifth Committee to the General Assembly under agenda item 107 appear in the reports of the Committee contained in documents A/59/652 and Add.1.
The Fifth Committee resumed its consideration of the item at its 57th meeting, on 8 June 2005.
Statements and observations made in the course of the Committee's consideration of the item are reflected in the relevant summary records (A/C.5/59/SR.57).
Consideration of draft decision A/C.5/59/L.78
At its 57th meeting, on 8 June 2005, the Committee had before it a draft decision entitled “Questions deferred for future consideration” (A/C.5/59/L.78), which was submitted by the Chairman.

(e) Report of the Advisory Committee on Administrative and Budgetary Questions (A/59/786);
(f) Report of the Secretary-General on a comprehensive assessment of the system of geographical distribution and assessment of the issues relating to possible changes in the number of posts subject to the system of geographical distribution (A/59/724).
