Fifty-ninth session
Agenda item 18
Election of judges of the International Tribunal for the Prosecution of Persons Responsible for Serious Violations of International Humanitarian Law Committed in the Territory of the Former Yugoslavia since 1991
Memorandum by the Secretary-General
Addendum
By a communication received on 16 August 2005, the Permanent Mission of Pakistan to the United Nations informed the Secretariat that Mr. Muhammad Muzammal Khan and Mr. Sheikh Abdul Rashid have decided to withdraw their candidatures.
In consequence, the names of the following candidates, which appear in paragraph 8 of document A/59/887, should be deleted from the list of candidates for ad litem judges of the International Tribunal:
Mr. Muhammad Muzammal Khan (Pakistan)
Mr. Sheikh Abdul Rashid (Pakistan)
