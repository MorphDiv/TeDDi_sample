Fifty-ninth session
Allocation of agenda items for the fifty-ninth session of the General Assembly
Addendum
At its 113th plenary meeting, on 14 July 2005, the General Assembly decided to consider directly in plenary meeting, under heading B (Promotion of sustained economic growth and sustainable development in accordance with the resolutions of the General Assembly and recent United Nations conferences), the following sub-item under item 85, which had been allocated to the Second Committee:
Sustainable development [item 85]:
(b) Further implementation of the Programme of Action for the Sustainable Development of Small Island Developing States.
At the same meeting, the General Assembly decided to add to the list of items to be considered directly in plenary meeting, under heading I (Organizational, administrative and other matters), the following item, which had been allocated to the Fifth Committee:
Scale of assessments for the apportionment of the expenses of the United Nations [item 113].
Subsequent entries considered directly in plenary meeting will be renumbered accordingly.
Therefore a total of 62 items are now to be considered directly in plenary meeting.
