Agenda of the sixtieth session of the General Assembly
Addendum
At its 26th plenary meeting, on 4 October 2005, the General Assembly, on the proposal of the Secretary-General (see A/60/231), decided to include the following item in the agenda of its sixtieth session under heading I (Organizational, administrative and other matters):
* Reissued for technical reasons.
Election of judges of the International Tribunal for the Prosecution of Persons Responsible for Serious Violations of International Humanitarian Law Committed in the Territory of the Former Yugoslavia since 1991.
