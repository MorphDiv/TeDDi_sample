Alignment
Modifies the alignment of the chart title.
Some of the options are not available for all types of labels.
For example, there are different options for 2-D and 3-D object labels.
Please note that problems may arise in displaying labels if the size of your chart is too small.
You can avoid this by either enlarging the view or decreasing the font size.
