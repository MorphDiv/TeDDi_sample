Enter the regular expression you want to search for here. Possible meta characters are:. - Matches any character ^ - Matches the beginning of a line $- Matches the end of a line \\lt; - Matches the beginning of a word \\gt; - Matches the end of a word The following repetition operators exist:? - The preceding item is matched at most once * - The preceding item is matched zero or more times + - The preceding item is matched one or more times {n} - The preceding item is matched exactly n times {n,} - The preceding item is matched n or more times {, n} - The preceding item is matched at most n times {n, m} - The preceding item is matched at least n, but at most m times. Furthermore, backreferences to bracketed subexpressions are available via the notation\\ n.
