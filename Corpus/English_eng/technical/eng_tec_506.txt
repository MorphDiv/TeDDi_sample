Indent
Specify the amount of space to leave between the left and the right page margins and the paragraph.
Before text
Enter the amount of space that you want to indent the paragraph from the page margin.
If you want the paragraph to extend into the page margin, enter a negative number.
In Left-to-Right languages, the left edge of the paragraph is indented with respect to the left page margin.
In Right-to-Left languages, the right edge of the paragraph is indented with respect to the right page margin.
