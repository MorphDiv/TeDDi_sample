Editing Curves
A curved line segment consists of two data points (endpoints) and two control points (handles).
A control line connects a control point to a data point.
You can change the shape of a curve by converting a data point to a different type, or by dragging the control points to a different location.
Format - Line

The data points are represented by squares and the control points by circles.
A control point might overlay a data point.
