Navigation
If you click this icon in the Navigator or in the lower right of the document window, a toolbar will appear which enables you to choose among the existing targets within a document.
You can then use the up and down arrow icons to position the text cursor in the document on the previous or next target.
Click the up button to scroll to the previous page or object.
Click the down button to scroll to the next page or object.
By default, as long as you have not selected any other entry, the arrow buttons jump to the previous or the next page in the document.
The arrow buttons are black if you are browsing through pages and blue if you jump to other objects.
