Slide Master
Switches to slide master view, in which you can add elements that you want to appear on all of the slides in your show that use the same slide master.
Inserts a new slide master into the document.
Double-click the new slide master on the Slides pane to apply it to all slides.
Select a slide master and click this icon to remove the slide master from the document.
Select a slide master and click this icon to rename the slide master.
Closes the slide master view.
