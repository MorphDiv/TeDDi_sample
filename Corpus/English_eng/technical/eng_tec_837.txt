Editing Chart Legends
To edit a chart legend:
Double-click on the chart.
A grey border appears around the chart and the menu bar now contains commands for editing the objects in the chart.
Format - Legend
or double-click on the legend.
This opens the
Legend
dialogue box.
OK
.
To select the legend, first double-click on the chart (see step 1), then click on the legend.
You can now move the legend within the chart using the mouse.
