Edit Sections
Changes the properties of sections defined in your document.
To insert a section, select text or click in your document, and then choose
Insert - Section
.
Edit Sections
dialogue box is similar to the
Insert - Section
dialogue box, and offers the following additional options:
Section
Section
list.
If the cursor is currently in a section, the section name is displayed on the right side of the status bar at the bottom of the document window.
The current write protection status of a section is indicated by the lock symbol in front of the section name in the list.
An open lock is unprotected and a closed lock is protected.
Similarly, visible sections are indicated by a glasses symbol.
Options
Options
dialogue box, with which you can edit the column layout, background, footnote and endnote behaviour of the selected section.
If the section is password protected, you must enter the password first.
Remove
Removes the selected section from the document, and inserts the contents of the section into the document.
