Monkey Bubble
bubble game
Introduction

Clear the jungle from all those nasty bubbles
To clear a level, you need to stick at least 3 bubbles of the same color together using the bubble shooter.
Once 3 or more bubbles of the color are sticked together at the top of the playground, they will explode.
To level up, you need to clear all the bubbles stuck in the playground.
