DataPilot
DataPilot
(sometimes known as
Pivot Table
) allows you to combine, compare, and analyse large amounts of data.
You can view different summaries of the source data, you can display the details of areas of interest, and you can create reports.
DataPilot
is an interactive table.
Data can be arranged, rearranged or summarised according to different points of view.
