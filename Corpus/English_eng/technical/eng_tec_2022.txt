Adding Line Numbers
$[officename] can automatically count and add line numbers to the lines in an entire document or to selected paragraphs in your document.
Line numbers are included when you print your document.
You can specify the line numbering interval, the starting line number, and whether to count blank lines or lines in frames.
You can also add a separator between line numbers.
Line numbers are not available in HTML format.
