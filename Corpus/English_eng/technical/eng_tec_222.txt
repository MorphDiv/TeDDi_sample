Callouts
Opens the Callouts toolbar from which you can insert graphics into your document.
Click an icon from the Callouts toolbar, then drag in the document to draw the shape.
Some shapes have a special handle which you can drag to change properties of the shape.
The mouse pointer changes to a hand symbol over these special handles.
