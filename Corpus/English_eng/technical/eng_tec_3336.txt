(PHP 4 = 4.0.6)
imagefilledarc() draws a partial ellipse centered at cx, cy (top left is 0, 0) in the image represented by image.
W and h specifies the ellipse 's width and height respectively while the start and end points are specified in degrees indicated by the s and e arguments. style is a bitwise OR of the following possibilities:
IMG_ARC_PIE
IMG_ARC_CHORD
IMG_ARC_NOFILL
IMG_ARC_EDGED
Note:
This function was added in PHP 4.0.6 and requires GD 2.0.1
