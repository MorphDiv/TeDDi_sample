Introduction
& klinkstatus; is a link checker for & kde;. It allows you to search internal and external links in your entire web site, just a single page and choose the depth to search. You can also check local files, ftp, fish, etc, as & klinkstatus; uses KIO. For performance, links can be checked simultaneously. Please report any problems or feature requests to http: / /linkstatus. paradigma. co. pt/ bugs/.
