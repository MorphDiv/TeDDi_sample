Fonts
Substitutes a font with a font of your choice.
The substitution only replaces a font when it is displayed on screen or during printing.
It does not change the font specified in your document formatting.
If you want, you can override the default substitution font that your operating system uses when it encounters an unavailable font in a document.
Font replacement also affects the display of fonts on the $[officename] user interface.
