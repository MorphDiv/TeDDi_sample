Basic Shapes
Opens the Basic Shapes toolbar which you can use to insert graphics into your document.
Click an icon on the Basic Shapes toolbar, and then drag in the document to draw the shape.
Some shapes have a handle which you can drag to change the properties of the shape.
The mouse pointer changes to a hand symbol over these special handles.
