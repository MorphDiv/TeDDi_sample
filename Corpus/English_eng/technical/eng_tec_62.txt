Importing and Exporting CSV Text Files with Formulae
Comma separated values (CSV) files are text files that contain the cell contents of a single sheet.
Commas, semicolons, or other characters can be used as the field delimiters between the cells.
Text strings are put in quotation marks, numbers are written without quotation marks.
