The fdf_get_ap() function gets the appearance of a field The fdf_get_ap() function gets the appearance of a field (i.e. the value of the / AP key) and stores it in a file.
The possible values of face are FDFNormalAP, FDFRolloverAP and FDFDownAP.
The appearance is stored in filename.
