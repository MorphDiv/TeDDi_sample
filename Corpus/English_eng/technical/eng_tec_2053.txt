Updating Styles From Selections
Format - Styles and Formatting
.
Click the icon of the style category that you want to update.
In the document, click where you want to copy the updated style from, for example, in a paragraph to which you applied some manual formatting that you want to copy now.
In the Styles and Formatting window, click the style that you want to update.
New Style from Selection
icon and choose
Update Style
from the sub-menu.
Only the manually formatted attributes of the text at the cursor position in the document will be added to the style that is selected in the Styles and Formatting window.
Any attributes that were applied as part of a style will not be added to the updated style.
