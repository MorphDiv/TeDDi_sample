Switching Between Insert Mode and Overwrite Mode
With the keyboard:
The text cursor must be enabled in the cell or in the input line.
With the mouse:
On the Status Bar, click on the area indicating the current mode in order to switch to the other mode:
INSRT
The text cursor is a blinking vertical line.
Click on the area to enable the overwrite mode.
OVER
The text cursor is a blinking block.
Click on the area to enable insert mode.
