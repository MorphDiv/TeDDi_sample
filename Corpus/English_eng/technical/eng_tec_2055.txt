Merging and Splitting Cells
You can select adjacent cells, then merge them into a single cell.
Conversely, you can take a large cell and divide it into individual cells.
To Merge Cells
Select the adjacent cells.
Table - Merge Cells
.
To Split Cells
Place the cursor in the cell to be split.
Table - Split Cells
.
A dialogue box allows you to split the cell into two or more cells, horizontally or vertically.
