Dragging and Dropping Within a $[officename] Document
There are many options for moving or copying objects using drag-and-drop.
Text sections, drawing objects, graphics, form controls, hyperlinks, cell ranges, and many more can be moved or copied with the mouse.
Note that the mouse pointer displays a plus sign when copying and an arrow when creating a link or hyperlink.
