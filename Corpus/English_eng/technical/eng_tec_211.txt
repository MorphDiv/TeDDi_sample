Default

Greyscale
The graphic object is shown in greyscale.
A colour graphic object can become monochrome in greyscale.
You can also achieve tinted monochrome colours by varying one of the colour parameters.

The graphic object is shown in black and white.
All brightness values below 50% will appear black, all over 50% will appear white.
