Editing Chart Titles
To edit a chart title that you have inserted into a $[officename] document:
Double-click on the chart.
A grey border appears around the chart and the menu bar now contains commands for editing the objects in the chart.
Double-click on an existing title text.
A grey border appears around the text and you can now make changes.
Press Enter to create a new line.
Insert - Title
to enter the text in a dialogue box.
A single-click on the title allows you to move it with the mouse.
