Changing the Outline Level of Numbered and Bulleted Lists
To move a numbered or bulleted paragraph down one outline level, click at the beginning of the paragraph, and then press Tab.
To move a numbered or bulleted paragraph up one outline level, click at the beginning of the paragraph, and then press Shift+Tab.
