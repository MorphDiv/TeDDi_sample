Removing Line Breaks
Use the AutoCorrect feature to remove line breaks that occur within sentences.
Unwanted line breaks can occur when you copy text from another source and paste it into a text document.
This AutoCorrect feature only works on text that is formatted with the &quot; Default &quot; paragraph style.
