Description
AMOR stands for Amusing Misuse Of Resources. It provides several different characters who prance around your X screen doing tricks and giving you tips.
& amor; is part of the & kde; toys package.
Options
& amor; is configured graphically, and does not have any commandline options other than the standard & kde; and & Qt; options.
See Also
Within & kde; you can enter help: / amor in & konqueror; to see the comprehensive user manual.
Bugs
& amor; currently only works with the & kwin; window manager.
