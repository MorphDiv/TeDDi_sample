Selecting Themes for Sheets
$[officename] Calc comes with a predefined set of formatting themes that you can apply to your spreadsheets.
It is not possible to add themes to Calc, and they cannot be modified.
However, you can modify their styles after you apply them to a spreadsheet.
Theme Selection
dialogue box.
To apply a custom cell style to a cell, you can open the Styles and Formatting window and, in its lower list box, set the Custom Styles view.
A list of the existing custom defined cell styles will be displayed.
Double-click a name from the Styles and Formatting window to apply this style to the selected cells.
To apply a theme to a spreadsheet:
Choose Themes
icon in the
Tools
bar.
Theme Selection
dialogue box appears.
This dialogue box lists the available themes for the whole spreadsheet and the Styles and Formatting window lists the custom styles for specific cells.
Theme Selection
dialogue box, select the theme that you want to apply to the spreadsheet.

Theme Selection

The modifications will be immediately visible in your spreadsheet.
