Changing Default Attributes
Can default formats in $[officename] Math be modified?
Some parts of formulae are always formatted bold or italic by default.
You can remove these attributes using &quot; nbold &quot; and &quot; nitalic &quot; .
Example:
a + b
nitalic a + bold b.
In the second formula, the a is not italic.
The b is bold.
You cannot change the plus sign by this method.
