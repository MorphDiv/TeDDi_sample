Turning Extended Tips On and Off
Extended tips
provide a brief description of the function of a particular icon, text box or menu command when you rest your cursor on that item.
To turn Extended Tips on and off:
Tools - Options - %PRODUCTNAME - General
, and check
Extended tips
.
A check mark indicates that the extended tips are activated.
To turn Extended Tips on temporarily:
Press the shortcut keys Shift+F1 to activate extended tips once.
