Inserting Page Numbers in Footers
You can easily insert a page number field in the footer of your document.
You can also add a page count to the footer, for example, in the form &quot; Page 9 of 12 &quot;
To Insert a Page Number
Insert - Footer
and select the page style that you want to add the footer to.
Insert - Fields - Page Number
.
If you want, you can align the Page Number field as you would text.
