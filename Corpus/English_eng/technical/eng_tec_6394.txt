Current features
& kamala; still is at a very early stage of its development. Many features and tools needed by astrologers nowadays are missing. But it is already possible to
display, edit, save and delete simple astrological charts
fine tune many calculation and drawing parameters
directly access the GeoNames online database
more to come: comparison charts, offline cities database, etc
