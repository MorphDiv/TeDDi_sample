Description
& ktouch; is a program for learning touch typing. It helps you learn to type on a keyboard quickly and correctly. Every finger has it's place on the keyboard with associated keys to press.
& ktouch; helps you learn to touch type by providing you with text to train on, and adjusts to different levels depending on how good you are. It can display which key to press next, and correct finger to use.
This application is part of the official & kde; edutainment module.
