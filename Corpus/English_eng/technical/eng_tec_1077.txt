Outline Numbering
You can modify the heading hierarchy or assign a level in the hierarchy to a custom paragraph style.
You can also add chapter and section numbering to heading paragraph styles.
By default, the &quot; Heading 1 &quot; paragraph style is at the top of the outline hierarchy.
