Zoom & View Layout
Three controls on the Writer Status Bar allow to change the zoom and view layout of your text documents.
The View Layout icons show from left to right:
Single column mode.
View mode with pages side by side.
Book mode with two pages as in an open book.
Drag the Zoom slider to the left to show more pages, drag to the right to zoom into a page and show a smaller area of the page.
