Changing the Slide Order
Do one of the following:
View - Slide Sorter
, select one or more slides, and then drag the slides to another location.
To select multiple slides, hold down shift and click on the slides.
To create a copy of a selected slide, hold down Ctrl while you drag.
The mouse pointer changes to a plus sign.
You can also drag a copy of a slide into another open $[officename] Impress document.
View - Outline
, select a slide, and then drag the slide to another location.
View - Normal
or
Notes
, select the slide preview on the
Slides Pane
, and then drag the slide preview to another location.
Slide Sorter
, right-click the slide, and then choose
Show/Hide Slide
.
The number of the hidden slide is crossed out.
To show the slide, right-click the slide, and then choose
Show/Hide Slide
.
