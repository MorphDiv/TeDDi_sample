To choose a chart type
chart type
: click any of the entries labelled Column, Bar, Pie, and so on.
The contents on the right side will change to offer more options depending on the basic chart type.
Optionally, click any of the options.
While you change the settings in the wizard, watch the preview in the document to see how the chart will look.
Shift+F1
and point to a control to see an extended help text.
Finish
on any wizard page to close the wizard and create the chart using the current settings.
Next
to see the next wizard page, or click the entries on the left side of the wizard to go to that page.
Back
to see the previous wizard page.
Cancel
to close the wizard without creating a chart.
