Creates an slob object and opens it.
Modes:
1 = LO_RDONLY, 2 = LO_WRONLY, 4 = LO_APPEND, 8 = LO_RDWR, 16 = LO_BUFFER, 32 = LO_NOBUFFER - or-mask.
You can also use constants named IFX_LO_RDONLY, IFX_LO_WRONLY etc.
Return FALSE on error otherwise the new slob object-id.
