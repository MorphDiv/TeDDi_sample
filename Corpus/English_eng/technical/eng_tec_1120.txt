Inserting a Scanned Image
To insert a scanned image, the scanner must be connected to your system and the scanner software drivers must be installed.
The scanner must support the TWAIN standard.
The Scanner must support the SANE standard.
Click in the document where you want to insert the scanned image.
Insert - Picture - Scan
, and choose the scanning source from the sub-menu.
Follow the scanning instructions.
