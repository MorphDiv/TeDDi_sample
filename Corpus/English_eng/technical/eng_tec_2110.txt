Value Highlighting
Highlights numerical values in your sheet.
To remove the highlighting, unmark the menu entry.
Text cells are formatted in black, number cells in blue and all other types of cells, such as formulae, logical values and dates, are formatted in green.
If this function is active, colours that you define in the document will not be displayed.
When you deactivate the function, the user-defined colours are displayed again.
