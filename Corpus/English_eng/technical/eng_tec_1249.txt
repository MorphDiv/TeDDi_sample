Insert Index/Table
Inserts an index or a table of contents at the current cursor position.
To edit an index or table of contents, place the cursor in the index or table of contents, and then choose
Insert - Indexes and Tables - Indexes and Tables

You can also preview the index or table in this dialogue box.
Depending on the type of index or table that you select, the following tabs are present.
Use this tab to specify the column layout for the index or table of contents.
