Adding Texture to Chart Bars
You can add texture to the bars in a graph or chart (instead of the default colours) via bitmap graphics:
Enter edit mode by double-clicking on the chart.
Double-click on any bar of the bar series that you want to edit.
All bars of this series are now selected.
If you want to edit only one bar, double-click again on that bar.
