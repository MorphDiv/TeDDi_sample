Finding and Replacing in Writer
In text documents you can find words, formatting, styles, and more.
You can navigate from one result to the next, or you can highlight all results at once, then apply another format or replace the words by other text.
The Find & Replace dialogue box
To find text within the whole document, open the Find & Replace dialogue box without any active text selection.
If you want to search only a part of your document, first select that part of text, then open the Find & Replace dialogue box.

The Navigator is the main tool for finding and selecting objects.
