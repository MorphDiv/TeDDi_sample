Hyphenation
By default, $[officename] moves words that do not fit on a line to the next line.
If you want, you can use automatic or manual hyphenation to avoid this behaviour:
Automatic hyphenation
Automatic hyphenation inserts hyphens where they are needed in a paragraph.
This option is only available for paragraph styles and individual paragraphs.
