Colour Window
In the two big colour windows, you click to select a new colour.
You can select the colour in the left or the right colour window as you wish.
In the right colour window, you will see the entire colour spectrum from the left to the right, with the colours at the top being fully saturated and the colours at the bottom being unsaturated.
In the left colour window, you will see a selection of colours, displaying a progressive spectrum, varying between the four colours in the four corners of this window.
You can change the colours in the four corners as follows:
Click the corner of the field in which you want to change the colour.
In the right colour window, click the desired new colour for the corner field or enter the values, which define the colour, in the numerical input fields.
