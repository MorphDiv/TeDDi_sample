Inserting and Deleting Page Breaks
To Insert a Manual Page Break
Click in your document where you want the new page to begin.
Press Ctrl+Enter.
To Delete a Manual Page Break
Click in front of the first character on the page that follows the manual page break.
Press Backspace.
