Rotating Objects
You can rotate an object around its default pivot point (centre point) or a pivot point that you designate.
Icon
Mode
toolbar in $[officename] Draw or on the
Drawing
bar in $[officename] Impress, click the
Rotate
icon.
Move the pointer to a corner handle so that the pointer changes to a rotate symbol.
Drag the handle to rotate the object.
Hold down the Shift key to restrict the rotation to multiples of 15 degrees.
Right-click the object to open the context menu.
Choose Position and Size - Rotation to enter an exact rotation value.
Icon
To change the pivot point, drag the small circle in the centre of the object to a new location.
To skew the object vertically or horizontally, drag one of the side handles.
