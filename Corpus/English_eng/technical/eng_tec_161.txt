Insert Cells
Insert Cells
dialogue box, with which you can insert new cells according to the options that you specify.
You can delete cells by choosing
Edit - Delete Cells
.
Selection
This area contains the options available for inserting cells into a sheet.
The cell quantity and position is defined by selecting a cell range in the sheet beforehand.
Shift cells down
Moves the contents of the selected range downwards when cells are inserted.
Shift cells right
Moves the contents of the selected range to the right when cells are inserted.
Entire row
Inserts an entire row.
The position of the row is determined by the selection on the sheet.
The number of rows inserted depends on how many rows are selected.
The contents of the original rows are moved downwards.
Entire column
Inserts an entire column.
The number of columns to be inserted is determined by the selected number of columns.
The contents of the original columns are shifted to the right.
